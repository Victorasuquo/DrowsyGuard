The Problem of Drowsiness

Drowsy driving is a serious and ongoing issue on our roadways. According to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA), fatigue-related collisions in the United States cause 1,550 deaths, 71,000 injuries, and more than $12.5 billion in economic losses each year - (NHTA, 2023).
